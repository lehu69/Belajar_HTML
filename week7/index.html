ABOUT

I am currently developing my expertise in Information Technology at Telkom University Jakarta, with a focus on software development and IoT device development. This interest drives me to continue improving myself, because I believe that consistency, enthusiasm for learning, and a never-give-up attitude are the keys to delivering useful innovations.

I actively participate in various projects that strengthen my understanding of technology. One of them is the development of the UI/UX for an online lending application called PinjamAja, which is designed to support the growth of the sharing economy. Additionally, I have expanded my experience through hardware and IoT development competitions, such as the Student Creativity Program (PKM) and Inovillage. From these experiences, I have learned how to combine creativity, technology, and the real needs of society.

My participation in projects and competitions deepened my technical expertise and honed my skills in teamwork, communication, and time management. I believe that blending technical and interpersonal abilities is vital for success in the professional world, particularly in a digital era that demands swift innovation and actionable solutions.

Some may assume that, as a student, my professional experience is limited. In reality, this stage of my life is where I am proactively building a powerful foundation by immersing myself in impactful projects, breakthrough research, and challenging competitions. This hands-on approach not only accelerates my growth but also makes me highly adaptable and exceptionally prepared to tackle new challenges with confidence and determination.

My enthusiasm for learning, expanding experience, and commitment to technology-based solutions position me to contribute meaningfully to software development and IoT. I view technology as a bridge to create a positive societal impact.
